about

jules granger is interested in visual language, built presentation, and authored work—projects that turn an idea into something people can stand near. two principles guide the practice: do all in the name of art, and let art be done by all.

through producing, design, and writing, jules builds environments and systems that help work become real: a poster that clarifies an idea, a room that holds attention, a process that carries a vision through.

the work is grounded in accessibility and materiality—art as meeting place, not escape hatch. the aim is to widen the canvas and make space for stories that would otherwise stay private.

education: b.a. in theatre arts & psychology — loyola university new orleans. currently pursuing an m.f.a. in creative producing & integrated media — calarts.
